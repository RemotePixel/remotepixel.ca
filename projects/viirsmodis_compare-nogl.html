MODIS-VIIRS Before/After

Modis-VIIRS Before/After is a tool to ease access to NASA MODIS and VIIRS imagery. Within a few click users can make comparison or before/after map and share it.

  1. Select left/right dates (click on LEFT and RIGHT buttons)
  2. Change MODIS-VIIRS product overlay
  3. Share it on Twitter/Facebook/Linkedin

Images are from EOSDIS Global Imagery Browse Services (GIBS) using excellent Alexander Parshin's leaflet-GIBS plugin to render on leaflet map.

Questions or comments to contact@remotepixel.ca

"I acknowledge the use of imagery provided by services from the Global Imagery Browse Services (GIBS), operated by the NASA/GSFC/Earth Science Data and Information System (ESDIS, https://earthdata.nasa.gov) with funding provided by NASA/HQ."
