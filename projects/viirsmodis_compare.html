MODIS-VIIRS Compare

Modis-VIIRS Compare is a tool to ease access and compare NASA MODIS and VIIRS imagery. Within a few click users can make comparison or before/after map and share it.

NASA Imagery from EOSDIS Global Imagery Browse Services (GIBS) using awesome Mapbox Mapbox GL to render and compare images.

Questions or comments to contact@remotepixel.ca

"I acknowledge the use of imagery provided by services from the Global Imagery Browse Services (GIBS), operated by the NASA/GSFC/Earth Science Data and Information System (ESDIS, https://earthdata.nasa.gov) with funding provided by NASA/HQ."
