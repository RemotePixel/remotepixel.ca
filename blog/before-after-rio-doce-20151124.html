Doce River after Samarco Mine Disaster

On Thursday, Nov. 5, Brazil had encounter maybe its worst ever environmental disaster. The Samarco iron mine failure buried the nearby town of Bento Rodrigues under 62 million cubic meters of toxic sludge and killed at least 9 (19 still missing).
