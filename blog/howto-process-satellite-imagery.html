Since the launch of my latest project Satellite Search, I have received a lot of positive messages from users around the globe and I'm really gratful for it.

This blog post is mostly to thank everyone who helped, encouraged or pushed me to keep on RemotePixel development. For more than a year now I've been trying to build tools that ease access and processing of Remote Sensing data and seeing how people are enjoying this project make me feel proud.

But, this is not an end, I feel there is a lot of work to do...

As usual I want to add a special thank to Mapbox and DevelopmentSeed. I couldn't have done so many projects without their API/Tools.
